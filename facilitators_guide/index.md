## More about this guide

Whether employed by a professional facilitator, people peer-facilitating each other, or a group that has no awareness of facilitation at all, Loomio‚Äôs essential function is to mediate and support group communication toward a desired outcome. In other words, facilitation.

Loomio is *groupware*:

> Collaborative software, or groupware, is designed to help people involved in a common task achieve their goals. One of the earliest definitions of collaborative software is ‚Äúintentional group processes plus software to support them.‚Äù -- Wikipedia


There is a huge body of knowledge, best practice, and tradition in both in-person facilitation and online moderation, but the art and science of their intersection is only just emerging. This is the specific space where Loomio strives to excel.
