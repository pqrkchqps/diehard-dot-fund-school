# Joining a Diehard.Fund group
Someone has invited you to join a Diehard.Fund group! Here are the essential things you can do to get off to a good start.

If you're not sure what Diehard.Fund is, check out ["What is Diehard.Fund?"](overview.md).

## Have a look around
Give yourself a tour by clicking around the app. Down the left is your *sidebar*, which gives you links to your groups, discussions, and decisions.

## Configure your email settings ğŸ“¬

Email notifications are the main way Diehard.Fund keeps you updated on what's happening in your group.

By default, we email you most of the activity on Diehard.Fund as it happens. This helps to keep people engaged in the early stages of a group, but can get overwhelming if there is a lot of activity.

You can be emailed about all activity, only important activity, or keep up to date with daily summary emails. Or you can turn off emails entirely. It's up to you.

Visit the [email settings](https://www.diehard.fund/email_preferences) to configure the email settings to suit your preferences. This page is linked from the sidebar.
